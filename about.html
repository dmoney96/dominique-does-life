About Me

Hello! I’m Dominique—a cybersecurity and OSINT professional passionate about helping people and organizations stay safe online. I co-founded BTech Solutions LLC to bring practical, affordable security services to small businesses in the Midwest.

With experience in GRC, threat analysis, and managed security services, I believe in blending empathy with rigor. Part of that rigor includes keeping our elected representatives accountable both to us as constituents, and to the United States Constitution itself. That is why, you will see collective action and lobbying content right here on the Dominique Does Life website, in addition to blog posts and podcast episodes. Whether I’m training a client team to spot phishing or diving into an OSINT investigation, I’m driven by the same principle: if nothing we do matters, then all that matters is what we do right now...today... -Tim Minear "Angel"

When I’m not knee-deep in code or intelligence gathering, you’ll find me lifting weights, practicing aerial yoga, or scambaiting fraudsters—legally and ethically, of course.
